Instructions

General Instructions

  • Students are instructed to come to the college only with neat formal dress and clean shave for boys and chudidhar for girls.
  • Cell phones are not allowed inside the campus.
  • Students participating in the symposium have to bring their college ID cards.
  • Students have to attend the symposium only by college bus transport. Click here for transport details.
  • Food will be provided inside the college.
  • Spot Registration for participation is not allowed.

Instructions for participants

  • The events will be conducted in two rounds- prelims and finals.
  • Students who are selected in prelims will be participating in finals.
  • Students can participate in multiple events
  • Students have to bring their paper presentations in CD and pendrive along with hard copy.
  • Students participating in the quiz can have maximum of 3 students per team.
  • Click here for quick registration or visit events section for more details on available events.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the food provided in college?

Yes, the college provides food on the day of symposium inside the campus

Will I get a certificate?

All the participants and winners get a certificate!

Is online registration mandatory?

Yes, online registration is mandatory and spot registrations may not be entertained.

What are timings of the symposium?

The symposium ends at 3:00 PM in the afternoon. Buses start by 3:30 PM.
