HONEKEN 2K18




About HONEKEN

If you are confident about your talent, do not be afraid to show it to the world. With such a note, the Electronics and communication Engineering Department of RMK Engineering College proudly presents to you HONEKEN 2K18, a National level electronic gathering .You can expect amazing technical and non-technical events here. We hope that our symposium will be an initiative to conceive various budding talents. Turn up, give the best in you and take away the best from us!

We have a total of 6 events: INCENDIO, CALICWIZ, FLOE, EVENTO SORPRESA, PROJECT EXHIBIT, ROBOTHON. Rock it!

EVENTS

INCENDIO

All you need to do is fuel your brain to ideate the ideas that never existed. The planet is all yours and come up with a spark of innovation to improve the lives around you. The stage has been set to listen to your words. Hope you fulfill the expectation in the form of PAPER PRESENTATION.

CALICWIZ

Quiz team competition is a pursuit of trivial Knowledge which encourage students to achieve academic excellence and increase their awareness of the world around them. Get ready for a roller coaster ride into the intense world of techno quizzing.

FLOE

Far from classroom rote, this quiz is all about the world of Science and Technology. Connections involve the use of innovative thinking and knowledge to get the answer right.

EVENTO SORPRESA

Event planners are coming up with new spins on the "surprise and delight" concept to make a memorable impact on guests. Adding an element of surprise to an event not only engages guests; it also makes events more memorable, and can help cement a brand's image as whimsical and forward-thinking.

PROJECT EXHIBIT

It does not take much strength to do things, but it requires a great deal of strength to decide what to do. Implement innovative ideas as projects to showcase your mind. Any innovative idea is acceptable.

ROBOTHON

The world of the future will be an even more demanding struggle against the limitations of our intelligence, not a comfortable hammock in which we can lie down to be waited upon by our robot slaves.
